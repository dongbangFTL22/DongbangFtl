Business Portfolio

As a pharmaceutical company, we need continuously evaluate not only our business Strategy but also disease strategy to discern gaps in our Portfolio. Business Portfolio allows it to pursue its stategic goals. This portfolio can also be defined as the set of available assets that the company posses to develop our mission and reach our goals.

Business Overseas

DongbangFTL has been exporting high quality pharmaceutical products to the big global pharmaceutical companies. We have many customers overseas such as Europe, Japan, China and many more. Main Business

Active Pharmaceutical Product:Our Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients factories specialize in most of anti-inflammatory analgesic drugs such as Mefenamic Acid, Loxoprofen Na and Felbinac and have successfully acquired the Austramilan GMP(TGA)certificate as well as the KGMP certificate.

Natural Product:Our Natural API plant has produced high quality natural API through the process of extraction, concentration, purification, drying since 1st approval of BGMP for Natural Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients in Korea in April 2004.

Formulation Product:Our Finished Drug plant is optimized in order to produce Soft / Hard Capsules, tablets The main strengths of ours are modern facilities, professional staffs and well-established quality assurance system through our strict manufacturing process management and quality control.
