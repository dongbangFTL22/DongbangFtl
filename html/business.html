Main Business

Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients

Our Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients factories specialize in most of anti-inflammatory analgesic drugs such as Mefenamic Acid, Loxoprofen Na and Felbinac and have successfully acquired the Austramilan GMP(TGA)certificate as well as the KGMP certificate.

In addition, we have obtained several certificates in regard of products such as Mefenamic Acid, Felbinac, Loxoprofen Na, Ketoprofen, Meloxicam, Propiverine HCI including European EDMF, Japanese JDMF and COS from french EDQM.

Natural Product

Our Natural API plant has produced high quality natural API through the process of extraction, concentration, purification, drying since 1st approval of BGMP for Natural Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients in Korea in April 2004. Also, we acquired GMP for natural active ingredient item by item in Korea in May 2010. We produce high quality natural API with the newest equipment under strict quality control. We have number of petents related to extraction and we collaborate with many different univercities, institutions and companies in order to develop new natural material.

Formulation Product

Our Finished Drug plant is optimized in order to produce Soft / Hard Capsules, tablets The main strengths of ours are modern facilities, professional staffs and well-established quality assurance system through our strict manufacturing process management and quality control. Our OEM(Original Equipment Manufacturing) products cover various therapeutic areas such as Ophthalmology, Circulation, Liver Disorder, Indigestion and Dermatological. We always do our best to satisfy our customers with high quality, convenience and best timing.
