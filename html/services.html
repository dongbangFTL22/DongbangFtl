Custom Synthesis Capabilities

CMC Consulting Services

Chemistry, Manufacturing and Controls Consulting services offer regulatory guidance for preparation of submissions required during various phases of drug development.

Consulting services for CMC regulatory submissions including:

  • INDs
  • NDAs
  • ANDAs
  • DMFs

Key CMC Consulting services offered:

  • Identification of PGIs (potential genotoxic impurity)
  • Reference standard characterization report
  • Gathering, reviewing and compiling of CMC information to ensure complete documentation required for the regulatory submissions
  • Quality checks to ensure the validity and consistency of CMC documentation and performing a gap analysis to identify any loopholes
  • Providing regulatory and strategic recommendations throughout the phases of drug development
  • Maintenance of global marketing licenses post-approval
