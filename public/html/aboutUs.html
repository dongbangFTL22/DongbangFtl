CEO Message

Thank You for visiting Dongbang Future Tech & Life Dongbang Future Tech & Life was originally founded under the name of Dongbang Synthetic Chemical and over the years we have built a trusted and renowned company through our patient centric pharmaceutical products. We at Dongbang Future Tech & Life believe in developing quality assured pharmaceutical products that contribute to the “health and wellness” of our consumers. With growing number of daily health related problems among the mankind around the world, we as a pharmaceutical company have a big responsibility on our shoulders to comply our focus more on quality based products that effectively benefit and relieve the patients. We believe in maintaining our income/operational cost ratio for achieving more stable growth and greater success in the pharmacological zone. We are proud to achieve KGMP certificates in the field of Finished Drugs, Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients and Natural Active Ingredients from the respective authorities in the past 20 years of our establishment. We are also well- known in the field of Total Health Care for developing Quasi Drugs and forming Cosmetic Factories and Research Institutes. With the use of natural and well-researched products, we at Dongbang Future Tech & Life believe in further growing our company globally by on-boarding various new skills, researches and technologies to instill in our ongoing and future projects for developing the most relied and trusted pharmaceutical products for our consumers and to spread improved health and happiness among them.

Thank you

CEO Hun Suk Chung
