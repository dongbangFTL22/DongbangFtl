Our History

1990 DongbangFTL has established in 1990 doing research and manufacturing pharmacutical products.
1991 We started exporting our products to Pfizer
1996 BGMP level natural API plant was constructed
1999 We got Authentication of Australian GMP (TGA)
2000 We got Authentication of BGMP for chemical API by Korea food and drug administration (KFDA)
2002 We started exporting to Japan.
Changed Company name to Dongbang Future Tech & Life Co. LTD
2004 We got Authentication of BGMP for herbal product by Korea food and drug administration (KFDA)
2006 Received Our First CEP authorized by EDQM
2008 Selected as export promising company (Small and medium business administration)
2011 Authentication of expertise specializing in parts, material by Ministry of Knowledge economy.
2013 We established a research company GAPI Bio
Authentication of Japanese GMP (PMDA)
2015 Selected as export promising company (Small and medium business administration)
2018 We got Authentication of EU-GMP(French ANSM)
2019 We established research company MFD Bio.
2020 We opened DongbangFTL new office in India
We established a Open Innovation Center
